Online and Local Cancer Support Communities

Here are some cancer support communities and resources where you can find help, share experiences, and connect with others affected by cancer:

  • American Cancer Society - Fight Cancer Together
  • Cancer Research UK - Cancer Chat
  • Cancer.Net - Support Groups
  • LIVESTRONG Foundation - Find Support
  • Pink Ribbon Foundation (Breast Cancer Support)
  • Ethiopian Cancer Society - Raising awareness, early detection, and treatment resources.
  • Addis Ababa University - Oncology Department - Providing cancer treatment and patient support.
  • Tikur Anbessa Specialized Hospital - Oncology Services - Offering cancer treatment and referrals.
  • Ethiopian Public Health Association - Promoting public health and cancer awareness in Ethiopia.

Join the Community

Additionally, you can connect with other cancer patients and survivors through these social media groups:

  • Ethiopian Cancer Support Group - Facebook
  • Cancer Awareness Ethiopia - Facebook

If you are looking for more personalized support, consider reaching out to your local healthcare providers or cancer specialists. Many hospitals and organizations offer resources for cancer patients and their families.
